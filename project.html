Integrated Projects

Together with my classmates, Kieren, Heidi, and Xandra, we developed a Smart Food Redistribution System as part of the Integrated Project (Ideation and Realisation) modules across Year 2 Semester 1 and 2. In the ideation phase, the team designed a prototype concept for a vending-machine-like system that collects surplus food, processes it safely, and redistributes it to communities in need. In the realisation phase, the project was further developed with hardware integration using Arduino Uno and C++ programming to demonstrate a more functional system. Although the project did not win at the final mini showcase, it provided valuable experience in prototyping, system development, and engaging with external visitors such as NExplorers and Shell representatives.
